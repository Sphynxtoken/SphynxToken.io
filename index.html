Why BSC?

Due to the Pulse network being a fork of Ethereum and not of BSC, we are able to create a variation of Sphynx that, based on interest and investment from community members and beyond, will determine the final token supply and distribution.
We do this by charging a 4% fee on every transaction.
Sphynx will not be duplicated over when the Pulse fork happens. Once Pulse mainnet has been launched, we will recreate Sphynx on Pulse and allow people to swap 1:1. Sphynx on Pulse will have different Tokenomics / Pumpermentals compared to Sphynx on BSC.
There will be no fee charged per transaction, the final 4% fee when swapping 1:1 will be reimbursed, and profts from our ventures will be used to buy back and burn Pulse Sphynx.
